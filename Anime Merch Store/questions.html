How can I place an order?

You can place an order by selecting the desired items, adding them to your cart, and proceeding to checkout.

Can I request for a customized design?

Yes, we accept customized design of the products.

Can I modify or cancel my order?

Once an order is placed, it cannot be modified or canceled. Please review your order carefully before confirming the purchase.

What are the shipping options?

We offer standard and express shipping options. Shipping costs and delivery times may vary based on your location and selected shipping method.

How can I track my order?

Once your order is shipped, you will receive a tracking number via email. You can use this tracking number to monitor the status and location of your package.

What is the return policy?

Our return policy allows you to return items within 30 days of purchase. Please refer to our Returns Policy for more details.

How do I initiate a return?

To initiate a return, contact our customer support at support@animemerchstore.com. Please provide your order number and reason for the return.
